\label{def:order_rel}

\index{ordering relation}
\index{ordered set}
%---------------------------------------
\defboxp{
  A relation $\orel$ is an \hid{partial ordering relation} on $\setX$ if
  \\$\begin{array}{@{\quad}ll@{\quad}C@{\quad}ED@{}r@{}E}
    \cline{6-6}
    1. & x \orel x
       & \forall x\in\setX
       & (\hi{reflexive})
       & and \hspace{2ex}
       & \text{ }\vline
       & \text{\hspace{2ex}preorder}
       \\
    2. & x \orel y \text{ and } y \orel z \implies x \orel z
       & \forall x,y,z\in\setX
       & (\hi{transitive})
       & and
       & \vline
    \\\cline{6-6}
    3. & x \orel y \text{ and } y \orel x \implies x=y
       & \forall x,y\in\setX
       & (\hi{antisymmetric})
       & 
       & 
  \end{array}$
  \\\\
  An \hid{partially ordered set} is the pair $\opair{\setX}{\orel}$. \\
  If $x\orel y$ or $y\orel x$, then we say that $x$ and $y$ are \hid{comparable};\\
  otherwise, they are \hid{incomparable} which is denoted as $x||y$.
  }

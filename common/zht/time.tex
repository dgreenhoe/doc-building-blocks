\section{Quantifiers for time}
%=======================================
\prefpp{voc:mw} already introduced a number of quantifiers for countable nouns.
Time, on the other hand, is uncountable.
It is either measured in standard units such as seconds, hours, days, etc.;
or it is described in more relaxed terms such as ``a moment".
Such quantifiers for time are listed in \pref{voc:mw_time} (next)
with examples provided in \prefpp{ex:mw_time}.

        \zhtsd{努}{力}{n/>u}{l/`i}{to exert}{force}{to strive}
\end{tblmw}
